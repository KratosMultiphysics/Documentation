Detailed Description

This is the Timoshenko curved beam element of 3 nodes. Reference: Connecting beams and continua: variational basis and mathematical analysis, Romero and Schenk, Meccanica, 2023 DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/s11012-023-01702-0.

Ordering of the nodes: 0 ---— 2 ----— 1

Quadratic interpolation of the curved geometry, displacements and rotation. Reduced integration to avoid shear locking

Author
Alejandro Cornejo
