Detailed Description

Defines a hyperelastic isotropic constitutive law in 2D Neohookean Model (Plane Strain) With stress split in an isochoric and volumetric parts This material law is defined by the parameters: 1) YOUNG MODULUS 2) POISSON RATIO As there are no further parameters the functionality is limited to large displacements elasticity.
